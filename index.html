About Corbin

Corbin, aka, Corbin Dallas, is my best friend. I adopted Corbin from the Buddy Center in January 2014 and he changed my life. He was a 10 month old, wild puppy with NO training and I wanted to be a dog trainer. He broke me, and I no longer want to be a dog trainer. We had to use 5 different trainers to get his behvior undeer control. He is now the most loving and well behaved (most days) dog I've ever had and adopting him was the best decison I ever made. Please use the link to the Buddy Center found at the bottom of the page to find out more about adopting a pet.
